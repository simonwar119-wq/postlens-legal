# PostLens Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 22, 2026**

This policy explains how PostLens ("we" or "us") handles information when you use our website and Chrome extension.

## Information we collect

- **Account data:** email address, optional name, and a securely hashed password.
- **Content you submit:** visible X post or thread text you explicitly ask PostLens to process, writing samples you provide for your Voiceprint, insights saved to your private Vault, creative briefs, and generated drafts.
- **Service data:** source URLs, character counts, model used, timestamps, subscription status, and limited security logs.
- **Payment data:** Lemon Squeezy processes payment details. We receive customer, subscription, and entitlement identifiers, not full card numbers.

## How we use information

We use data to build your private Voiceprint and Vault, provide AI summaries and drafts, authenticate users, enforce plan limits, process subscriptions, prevent abuse, troubleshoot, and comply with law. We do not sell personal information or use submitted content to train shared PostLens models.

## AI processing

Text you choose, relevant Voiceprint rules, selected Vault sources, and recent drafts may be sent securely to our server and then to DeepSeek solely to generate your requested output or Voiceprint. Do not submit sensitive personal information. Processing may occur outside your country.

## Chrome extension access

The extension accesses visible page content only on `x.com` and `twitter.com` so it can extract the post or thread you select. It does not access direct messages, cookies, passwords, browsing history, or unrelated sites. It never publishes automatically.

## Retention and security

One-off text submitted only for summarization is processed transiently. Voice samples, Voiceprint rules, saved insights, their source links, and recent drafts are stored so your private context can improve future results. You can remove Vault items individually and request full account deletion. Account and subscription records remain while your account is active and as required for legal or fraud-prevention purposes.

We use encryption in transit, access controls, password hashing, and least-privilege infrastructure. No system is completely secure.

## Your choices

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your account and associated personal information. You can remove the extension at any time and manage billing through the customer portal.
